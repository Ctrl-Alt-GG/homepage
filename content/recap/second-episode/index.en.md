Nearly fifty of us gathered in Biatorbágy, and the second Ctrl‑Alt‑GG finally delivered that classic LAN atmosphere we’d all been waiting for. We cranked everything up a notch: the network was upgraded, the lineup expanded, and the crowd was buzzing all weekend.

A particularly hardcore crew even spun up a Factorio server and, in the 24 hours they had, absolutely tore through the entire game. For the first time we also managed a proper Counter‑Strike 2 tournament: the group stage turned into a brutal playoff, and the best‑of‑three final was an exciting two-map affair. Big congrats to the winners - they truly deserved it.

Of course, that wasn’t all. Someone built an industrial empire in OpenTTD, TF2 had people in stitches non‑stop, and the trusty CS: Source and LoL scenes were running at full speed. By the end everyone - organizers included - collapsed from exhaustion, but the weekend was such a success we honestly could’ve kept going for days.
