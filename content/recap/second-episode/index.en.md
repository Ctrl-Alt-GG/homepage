For the second Ctrl-Alt-GG event, unpacking PCs, monitors, and cables already felt a little more familiar, but the atmosphere was just as fresh as the first time. The room quickly filled with conversation, keyboard clicks, and the shared anticipation of another evening focused on games and good company.

Once the machines were up and running, people naturally split into smaller groups: some prepared for the next match, while others chased the fun of lighter games and shared laughs. The best moments again came from the spontaneous little scenes — someone calling across the room, laughter after a great round, or a quick technical rescue turning into a group effort.

This short gallery is only a first taste of what the latest meetup felt like. The full recap can still be rewritten and polished later, but until then these photos keep a few reminders of why we enjoy this small LAN community so much.
